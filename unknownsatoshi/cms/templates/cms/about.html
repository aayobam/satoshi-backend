Who we are?

All-in-One Community Where You Learn All About Cryptocurrency.

Unknown Satoshi is a learning community which helps you understand the basics of cryptocurrencies and expand your knowledge of blockchain technology, gives you access to up to date market analysis and expose you to tools and resources you can leverage on to better navigate the crypto market.

Our Mission

Grow together, Learn together and Earn together

Our mission is to create a community focused platform which educates and empowers you with the right crypto knowledge and tools for you to become a profitable trader and investor. We do that through our community, trainings, courses, physical events and other innovative crypto ideas.

Our Core Values

In order to achieve our mission, we believe we must embody a philosophy that guides us, and these are values we represent as a brand
  • New and innovative crypto ideas
  • Crypto jargon simplified
  • Teamwork
  • Community obsessed
  • Decentralized, peer 2 peer and borderless
  • Financial freedom
